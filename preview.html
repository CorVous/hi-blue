// Hypothesis refinements (after reading the code)

content-pack-generator.ts:647 calls rollObjectiveTypes(rng, 3) unconditionally. The kRange: [1, 2] field in phases.ts is completely ignored by the dual-pack path. Always exactly 3. My three candidate objectives were the right count — lucky guess.

Setting Shift and Chat Lockout confirmed as complications. Both in complication-engine.ts, not complications.ts. Setting Shift gated by !settingShiftFired boolean. Chat Lockout has no cooldown — consecutive draws valid, explaining back-to-back lockouts at turns 94 and 100. Obstacle Shift and Sysadmin Directives remain unconfirmed but strongly hypothesized.
H3 — Persona traits  confirmed
typing-quirk-pool.ts (41 entries) confirmed exact matches for all three observed quirks. persona-goal-pool.ts matched all three persona goals precisely (goals #11, #12, #14).
